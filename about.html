About Ryan Branch

Hi! I'm Ryan Branch, a software engineer and scientific researcher from Michigan.

My hobbies include 3D printing, electronics, and programming. I have a wide variety of software interests including simulations, image processing, machine learning, procedural generation, and digital art.

Undergraduate Education

I attended the University of Michigan College of Engineering from September 2014 to April 2018.

During this time I participated heavily in scientific research, which you can read about here.

In April of 2018 I graduated with a B.S.E. in Chemical Engineering and a minor in Computer Science. Further educational details, including a list of coursework, are available here.

Employment

After graduating, I first worked as an Automation Engineer in the biopharmaceutical industry.

Since May of 2020 I have been a Software Engineer at Photon Semantics, a computer vision startup which I co-founded in 2017 along with several other researchers and professors.



Graduate School Applications

I am currently applying to Ph.D. programs, with the goal of studying Computational Materials Science beginning in the Fall 2021 semester.

If you are visiting this site as a part of reviewing my application to your school, you may find my Portfolio page to be especially helpful.
